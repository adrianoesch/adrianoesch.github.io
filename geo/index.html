Additional Layers for geo.admin

I've scraped some websites for their valueable information, put them in a kml file, which then can be imported into the geo.admin page

Some of the links are outdated, since the topo institute has released official data (ski routes for example).

Use with caution, nothing guaranteed, call in advance!
